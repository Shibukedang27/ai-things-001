# OfferPilot AI Commercial Applications

OfferPilot AI can serve multiple commercial markets where interview readiness, candidate assessment, and skill development have measurable business value.

## Target Segments

| Segment | Use Case |
| --- | --- |
| Individual job seekers | Practice interviews, coding preparation, resume improvement, personalized study plans |
| Bootcamps | Student readiness tracking, mock interview automation, placement preparation |
| Universities | Career services, technical interview labs, graduate employability analytics |
| Recruiting agencies | Candidate screening preparation, resume gap analysis, shortlist quality improvement |
| Enterprise hiring teams | Internal mobility, structured interview practice, role-readiness programs |
| EdTech platforms | Embedded AI interview preparation as a value-added module |
| Coaching businesses | Scalable practice, reports, and homework plans between human coaching sessions |

## Product Packages

| Package | Audience | Features |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Individual users | Limited interviews, basic dashboard, sample resume scan |
| Pro | Serious job seekers | Unlimited practice, live coding, resume analyzer, AI evaluations, roadmaps |
| Team | Bootcamps and universities | Cohorts, leaderboard, analytics, admin dashboards |
| Enterprise | Companies and agencies | SSO, tenant isolation, audit logs, custom rubrics, data controls |
| API | Platforms | Embeddable interview, evaluation, resume, and recommendation endpoints |

## Revenue Models

- Monthly subscription for individuals.
- Annual licenses for teams.
- Per-seat pricing for bootcamps and universities.
- Enterprise contracts with onboarding and support.
- Usage-based API pricing.
- Premium AI credits for high-volume evaluations.

## Differentiators

- Combines interview practice, answer evaluation, live coding, resume analysis, and learning plans in one product.
- Supports both technical and behavioral preparation.
- Produces structured analytics rather than one-off feedback.
- Can serve individuals and organizations from the same product architecture.
- Provider abstraction enables future AI model flexibility.

## Business Metrics

Recommended metrics to track:

- Activation rate after signup.
- Interviews completed per user.
- Evaluation completion rate.
- Roadmap follow-through rate.
- Resume analyzer conversion rate.
- Weekly active users.
- Retention by segment.
- Paid conversion rate.
- Team cohort readiness improvement.
- AI cost per active user.

## Enterprise Value Proposition

For organizations, OfferPilot AI can reduce preparation gaps, standardize coaching, and make readiness measurable. Enterprise buyers may value:

- Admin dashboards.
- Role-specific readiness analytics.
- Custom question banks.
- Candidate development reports.
- Security and privacy controls.
- API integration with existing learning platforms.

## Ethical and Compliance Considerations

- Communicate that AI feedback is advisory.
- Avoid using AI scores as the only hiring decision input.
- Provide bias review for generated feedback.
- Support data deletion and export workflows.
- Preserve transparency around resume and transcript processing.
